Bio

Hello! I joined UIUC as a graduate student in 2021, working with Professor Haitham Hassanieh in the SENS lab and moved to EPFL in January of 2023 to continue my PhD. I grew up in the Champaign-Urbana cornfields in central Illinois, but have lived in Taipei and Palo Alto for brief periods of time in the past. Currently, I am located in Lausanne, Switzerland. I received my B.S. in Computer Engineering from UIUC in May 2021 and my M.S. in ECE from UIUC in December 2022.

Research

During my undergraduate career, I explored research and coursework in the field of sensors, circuits, power electronics, machine learning and wireless communications. Currently, I work in the area of wireless sensing and networking. Primarily, my recent work has been in wireless imaging.
